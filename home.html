History

The Club moves with the times while honouring its traditions and the women who made possible the freedoms we take for granted today. Events with prominent guest speakers remain highlights on the Club calendar and our Circles.It was founded by a group of women who refused to allow the restrictions placed on them to stand in the way of their determination to fully participate in public life.
